Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Echo helps creators turn one idea into a multi-platform content kit in their own brand voice. This policy explains exactly what happens when you upload a posts file you exported from a social platform so Echo can learn your voice from your own past posts.

The short version

  • No login, ever — you upload a file you exported yourself; Echo never connects to your account.
  • Your file never leaves your device — it's read and parsed in your browser, not uploaded to us.
  • We read it once, to set up your brand voice — a few short writing samples.
  • Those samples live only on your device, in your browser's local storage.

What you upload

You export your own data from the platform and upload a single file — the one that contains your posts:

  • Instagram: the posts_1.json file from your "Download your information" export — we read only your captions.
  • X: the tweets.js file from your data archive — we read only your posts' text (retweets and replies are dropped).
  • LinkedIn: the Shares.csv file from "Get a copy of your data" — we read only the post text.

We never ask for your password and never connect to your account. The file is processed entirely in your browser.

How your data is handled

  • Your uploaded file is read and parsed in your browser — it is never uploaded to our servers, never written to a database, and never logged.
  • Your posts are cleaned into up to four short writing samples that stay on your device. Those samples — your "brand voice" — are stored only in your browser's local storage, so they survive refreshes.
  • We do not sell or share your data, and we do not use it for advertising or to train models on your behalf.

Generating content

When you generate a content kit, your brief and your brand-voice samples are sent to our synthesis endpoint to produce the kit, which is returned to your browser. We do not retain these requests to build a profile of you.

Your choices & deleting your data

  • There's nothing to revoke — Echo never connects to your account, so it holds no access to remove.
  • Because your brand voice lives in your browser, you can clear it instantly from within Echo (clear the posts field) or by clearing your browser storage for this site.
  • We hold none of your data on our servers, so there is nothing for us to delete on your behalf.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email you@example.com.
